How to Play
1
The board is a 3×3 grid of mini boards. Win three mini boards in a row to win the game.
2
Your move inside a mini board determines which mini board your opponent must play in next — the cell you pick maps to the corresponding board position.
3
The highlighted boards show where you're allowed to play this turn. If you're sent to an already-won board, you may play anywhere.
4
Win a mini board by getting three in a row — just like normal tic-tac-toe. A drawn mini board is locked and can't be won.
5
The first player to claim three mini boards in a row wins. Think ahead — every move shapes the battle!
